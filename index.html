Renting An Apartment in Germany: A Difficult Mission One of the most difficult things you have to do when you arrive in Germany is to rent an apartment. If you happen to like it, you make a deposit, pay the rent for the first month – and you can move in. Yes, that’s how things work in other places, but not in Germany. In Germany, that includes a little more complex procedure. And we have to say, a much harder one.
